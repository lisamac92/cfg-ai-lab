## Key Observations
- The current site is a traditional marketing agency site
- The AI Lab (index.html) is dramatically more modern, interactive, and impressive
- The current site does NOT showcase the AI Lab or interactive demos
- The 5Fs framework is the strategic backbone but is presented statically
- The AI Lab's three tables (Receptionist, Prospecting, Workflows) map directly to the 5Fs
- There is a disconnect: the current site promises AI but doesn't demonstrate it
- The AI Lab IS the proof — it needs to be the centrepiece of the main site
